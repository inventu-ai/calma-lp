Know before you go

Find answers to common questions and get ready for your fitness journey!

Learn More

What are your gym hours?

We’re open Monday–Friday from 5 AM to 10 PM and Saturday–Sunday from 7 AM to 8 PM.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes! Our certified trainers provide personalized workout plans to help you reach your goals. Contact us to book a session.

What membership options do you offer?

We have flexible memberships, including monthly, annual, and day passes. Visit our pricing page for details.

Are group fitness classes included in my membership?

Yes! All standard memberships include access to our group fitness classes like yoga, HIIT, and spin.

Do you have locker rooms and showers?

Yes, we provide clean and spacious locker rooms with showers and towel service for your convenience.
